Urban Legends

2023 2h 15min Mystery, Thriller PG-13

In the heart of a bustling metropolis, a group of skeptical college students embark on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the city's most infamous urban legends. As they delve deeper into the mysteries, the line between fact and fiction blurs, and they soon find themselves living the very stories they once dismissed as mere myths. From the director of "Midnight Whispers" comes a gripping tale that will keep you on the edge of your seat. "Urban Legends" masterfully weaves together suspense, humor, and heart-pounding action, challenging viewers to question what they believe and reminding us that sometimes, the most unbelievable stories hold the deepest truths. This critically acclaimed film has been praised for its innovative storytelling, stunning visual effects, and breakout performances from its young cast. Prepare to be captivated by a world where folklore comes to life and every shadow holds a secret waiting to be revealed.
