Quantum Leaps

TV-MA 2023 2 Seasons

Quantum Leaps is a mind-bending sci-fi series that explores the concept of parallel universes and the consequences of interdimensional travel. Dr. Samantha Wells, a brilliant quantum physicist, accidentally discovers a way to leap between realities. Each episode follows her attempts to navigate unfamiliar versions of her life while trying to find her way back to her original timeline. The show delves into complex scientific theories, ethical dilemmas, and the butterfly effect of small changes across multiple dimensions.
