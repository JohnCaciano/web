Midnight Mysteries

2023 | TV-MA | 2 Seasons | Mystery

Step into the foggy streets of Victorian London, where the line between the natural and supernatural blurs. Follow the brilliant yet troubled Detective Evelyn Blackwood as she unravels a series of perplexing cases that defy rational explanation. With her trusted partner, the skeptical Dr. James Harlow, they navigate a world where science and the occult collide, facing off against dark forces that threaten the very fabric of reality.
