Buckle up for the adrenaline-fueled thrill ride of the year! "Velocity Surge" is a high-octane racing thriller that seamlessly blends the world of professional racing with a gripping tale of international espionage. Follow the journey of Alex Chase, a talented but disgraced former race car driver, as he's recruited by a covert government agency to infiltrate an underground racing syndicate suspected of funding global terrorism.

As Alex navigates treacherous hairpin turns and high-stakes poker games, he uncovers a conspiracy that goes far beyond the racetrack. With cutting-edge car chases filmed across five continents and a star-studded cast delivering powerhouse performances, "Velocity Surge" pushes the limits of the action genre, delivering a perfect balance of heart-pounding excitement and sophisticated storytelling.
